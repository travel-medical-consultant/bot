Conversational Agent Research Study

Introduction

Dear participant,

Thank you for participating in this study. This study is part of a coursework for the course Collaborative Development of Conversational Agents organized by four German Universities, namely Karlsruher Institut für Technologie, Technische Universität Dresden, Universität des Saarlandes and Universität Hamburg.

The purpose of this study is to investigate the difference in the impact of chatbots with and without human-like features on users. The study takes about 20 minutes and consists of three parts. During the whole process, you do not have the possibility to go back to the previous part. Once you click “Next”, you will be forwarded to the next section and cannot go back. We recommend to use a laptop for this survey. As the learning material contains complex English terms and explanations, you need to have at least a B2 English level to take part.

Instructions

Thank you for taking part in the research study "Collaborative Development of Conversational Agent". Please read the instructions carefully. The following survey consists of three main parts.

Pre-survey

In this section, you will be asked about your travel and chatbot use experience, followed by a multiple choice single answer Knowledge Retention Test. Please make sure to submit the Google form before proceeding to the next section.

Chat with Chatbot

In this part, you will chat with a travel medical consultant chatbot. For this task, you can consider any of the following three scenarios.
  • You are traveling to Australia from any other country.
  • You are traveling to Sudan from any ther country.
  • You are traveling to Ethiopia from any other country.
We recommend you to consider two or more scenarios. To restart the chat, you can message `/restart` to the chatbot. We recommend you to chat with the chatbot for at least two minutes.

Post-survey

In this section, you will be asked questions related to your experience in using the chatbot. You will also be asked to retake the Knowledge Retention Test. Please make sure to submit the Google form before proceeding to the next section.
